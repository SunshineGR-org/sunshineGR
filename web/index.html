SunshineGR

SunshineGR is a US-based, non-profit organization (501c3) dedicated to improving access to public data published by the Greek government. Our mission is to empower citizens, journalists, and politicians, and promote transparency by making government data easily accessible and understandable.

Our first initative helps candidates for the upcoming 2023 elections analyze the decisions of their local administrations. For more information on SunshineGR, visit our FAQ ( English, Greek ).
